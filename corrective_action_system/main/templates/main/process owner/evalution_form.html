Evaluation of Internal Auditor

1st Sem of A.Y. 2024-2025

Instruction: Assess the performance of the internal auditor, taking into account the specified areas identified by the indicators provided for each category. Use the scale provided below for your evaluation. We also encourage you to provide comments in each area as additional inputs for improvement.

  • 5 – Excellent: Outstanding performance; Consistently surpasses expectations, demonstrating exceptional competence
  • 4 – Above Average: Exceeds expectations; Demonstrates strong proficiency and contributes positively
  • 3 – Average: Meets expectations; Competent performance with room for improvement
  • 2 – Below Average: Below expectations; Some improvement needed in this area
  • 1 – Poor: Significantly below expectations; Requires substantial improvement and additional training

    • Ensure that you complete all sections of the evaluation to provide a comprehensive assessment.
